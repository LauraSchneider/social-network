Key Characteristics of Current Social Media Policies at U.S. Allopathic Medical Schools
Amanda Xi*, Joel Topf*, Jill Stefaniak

Background It is estimated that 90% of medical students participate in online social networking. Although social media sites offer unique avenues for communication and new opportunities for education they may open students to unrecognized potential patient privacy violations and damage professional reputation.
